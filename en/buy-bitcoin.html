What Is Bitcoin and Why Buy It with Local Currency

₿ Bitcoin — digital gold with a fixed supply

Bitcoin (BTC) was created in 2009 as the world's first decentralised currency. Its core properties make it a unique financial tool for people in emerging markets:

  • 🔒 Fixed supply of 21 million BTC — no government or central bank can print more
  • 🌐 Decentralised — no single country controls it, no government can freeze it
  • 🔑 Your wallet, your Bitcoin — only you control your private keys
  • 📈 Historical long-term growth — despite short-term volatility, Bitcoin has appreciated significantly over multi-year horizons
  • 🌍 Globally accepted — sellable on any exchange in the world, accepted as payment by thousands of merchants
  • ⚡ Divisible to 8 decimal places — you can buy as little as 1 satoshi (0.00000001 BTC), no need to buy a whole coin
