1. What Data We Collect

CryptoSwap Bot does not require registration and does not request personal data. During use of the bot, the following may be technically recorded:

  • Telegram user ID (for session identification)
  • Exchange request history (not linked to any identity)
  • IP address (only when accessing the website; logs are stored for 7 days)

2. How We Use Data

Collected technical data is used exclusively for:

  • Ensuring the Service operates correctly
  • Error analysis and improving user experience
  • Prevention of abuse and spam

3. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell or share personal data. Technical logs may be shared with partner providers (ChangeNOW, SimpleSwap) solely to the extent necessary to execute an exchange, in accordance with their privacy policies.

4. Cookies and Analytics

The website uses:

  • localStorage — to save your selected interface language
  • sessionStorage — to prevent redirect loops when switching languages
  • Anonymized cookies for basic visit analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. This will not affect the core functionality of the site.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your technical data by contacting support: @CryptoSwapBot_support

6. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update this policy. The current version is always available at this address.
