Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. What Data We Collect

CryptoSwap Bot does not require registration and does not request personal data. During the use of the bot, the following technical information may be recorded:

  • User's Telegram ID (for identification within the session)
  • Exchange request history (without linking to identity)
  • IP address (only when accessing the website, logs are kept for 7 days)

2. How We Use the Data

Collected technical data is used exclusively for:

  • Ensuring the functionality of the Service
  • Analyzing errors and improving user experience
  • Preventing abuse and spam

3. Data Transfer to Third Parties

We do not sell or transfer personal data. Technical logs may be transferred to partner providers (ChangeNOW, SimpleSwap) exclusively to the extent necessary to execute the exchange, in accordance with their privacy policies.

4. Cookies and Analytics

The website may use anonymized cookies for basic traffic analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

You can request the deletion of your technical data by contacting support: @CryptoSwapBot_support

6. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update this policy. The current version is always available at this address.
