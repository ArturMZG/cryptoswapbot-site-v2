Why KYC Is a Problem for Users in Africa and Asia

📋 What KYC Actually Requires — and Why It Fails Many Users

Standard KYC on major exchanges includes:

  • 📷 Government-issued ID scan — passport or national ID front and back
  • 🤳 Selfie with ID — live photo holding your document
  • 🏠 Proof of address — utility bill or bank statement dated within 3 months
  • 📱 Phone number verification — must match country of registration
  • ⏳ Wait time — from hours to weeks, often rejected without explanation

For users in Nigeria, India, Ghana, Philippines and similar markets, KYC creates specific problems:

  • ❌ IDs not recognized by foreign platforms — Nigerian NIN, Indian Aadhaar often rejected
  • ❌ Country restrictions — some exchanges block entire countries
  • ❌ Data stored abroad — your passport scan on foreign servers
  • ❌ Account frozen at any time, funds inaccessible for weeks
  • ❌ Data breaches — major exchanges hacked regularly
